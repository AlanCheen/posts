## [Pidcat](https://github.com/JakeWharton/pidcat)
JakeWharton大神的logcat辅助工具,只显示你要看的进程,更加美观,体验更好.  

用法很简单:  
```
pidcat packagename
```

比如我要看网易云音乐的日志:  
```Shell
pidcat com.netease.cloudmusic
```
